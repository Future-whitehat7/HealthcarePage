How it can Spread

Influenza aka "flu" is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. it can cause mild to severe illness, and can sometimes lead to death.

When a person gets infected with the virus, the virus will begin replicating in their system.
If they have only absorbed a small quantity of virus and their immune system is strong then their system will produce enough antibodies to quickly stop the replication and person will either be asymptomatic or only get mildly ill for a short time.

Contaminated Objects

If a person comes in contact witha contaminated object it can cause mild to severe illness.

Air bourne transmission

By an infected individual sneezing, blowing their nose and coughing without covering their mouth or nose can transmit into the air around you.

Transmission amongs humans

Coming in contact with someone such as hugging, kissing or shaking hands is a way for the flu to be transmitted.

Prevention Tips from CDC

  • Clean your hands often.
  • Don't touch your eyes, nose or mouth.
  • Keep 5-10 feet away from others when possible.
  • If you feel sick, stay home when you can.
  • Listen for orders from your local government to stay home.
  • Cover coughs and sneezes with your elbow.
